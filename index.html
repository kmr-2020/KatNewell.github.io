About Art 9ine studios

Hi, my name in Katherine Newell. Art 9ine studios is an expression of my creative side and a dream for my future. I started out as a studio artist experimenting in mixed medium art. My focus has always been on drawing. I now find myself reaching out into the world of programming and web develop. I view programming is just on other medium though which I can create. Take a look at my projects page and see some of my artwork as well as some links to web pages I have created.
